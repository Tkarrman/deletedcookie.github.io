Just Around the Corner

First test of dragging a villager using a ragdoll-based system.

2026-05-31 Milestone: Pre-Production Category: Feature testing

What We Worked On

We experimented with a draggable ragdoll where players can grab a villager by a limb and pull them through the environment. The current version forces the player to walk backwards while dragging, making it harder to see what is behind them and creating opportunities for surprise encounters.
