The Game

A village is dying. You are here to help. Probably.

Chaotic co-op in a dark, funny medieval world.

Save villagers from disease before your own treatments become the real problem.

Vision

Three pillars drive every system.

1
Chaotic cooperation

Investigate, argue, carry, craft, treat, panic.

2
Dark humor

Medieval horror with terrible medical confidence.

3
Consequences

Every cure can become the next disaster.

Core Loop

Diagnose. Gather. Mix. Treat. Regret.

Examine symptoms, collect ingredients, brew medicine, treat villagers.

Time pressure, limited resources, and voice chat do the rest.

Features

Systems that create memorable disasters.

Investigate Symptoms

Read symptoms before the outbreak spreads.

Gather and Brew

Combine ingredients in a shared cauldron.

Surgery and Treatments

Medicine, surgery, quarantine, and panic.

Dragging and Disposal

Move bodies before they become a bigger issue.

Why It Is Different

Players try to solve problems. Every solution can make things worse.

The medicine works.

Or it poisons the patient.

The amputation stops the infection.

Or you cut off the wrong leg.

The corpse is probably fine.

Or it becomes the start of the next catastrophe.

Market Opportunity

Social co-op runs on stories.

Players remember the session, not the perfect strategy.

Plague Crew adds a strong fantasy: plague doctors, witches, rats, disease, folklore, and bad medicine.
