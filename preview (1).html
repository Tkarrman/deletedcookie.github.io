Plague Crew

The plague is the easy part.

A first-person chaotic co-op survival comedy/horror game about tiny plague doctors trying to save infected villages before everything goes horribly wrong.

1–4 Player Co-op Medieval Horror-Comedy Emergent Chaos PEGI 12 Target
The Game

Save the village. Or make it worse.

Players travel between infected villages, diagnose symptoms, gather resources, craft questionable medicine, perform desperate treatments, and manage the consequences together.

Chaotic Cooperation

Funny, stressful situations created by communication, panic, and bad decisions.

Dark Humor

Creepy medieval horror mixed with absurd comedy and stylized presentation.

Consequences

Mistakes affect villagers, reputation, outbreaks, monsters, and the village state.

Characters & World

Tiny doctors. Big problems.

Players are tiny plague doctors summoned by a helpful witch. Villagers show symptoms through appearance and behavior, forcing the group to diagnose, prioritize, and improvise.

Features

Designed for memorable co-op stories.

  • Investigation and diagnosis
  • Resource gathering in dangerous areas
  • Shared cauldron medicine crafting
  • Improvised combat and survival
  • Surgery and extreme treatments
  • Dragging bodies and items
  • Dynamic events: Rat King, infestations, outbreaks
  • Stealth with barrels, hay, grass, and distractions
  • The Hub: a strange walking Baba Yaga-inspired home
  • Progression through tools, upgrades, and replayable chaos
Market Position

Social chaos with a strong hook.

Plague Crew fits the growing audience for replayable co-op games built around communication, panic, and streamer-friendly moments.

Comparable Audience

Fans of Lethal Company, R.E.P.O., Content Warning, Phasmophobia, Overcooked, and social co-op chaos games.

Unique Fantasy

Medieval plague doctors, chaotic treatments, infected villages, dark humor, and consequence-driven systems.

Shareable Moments

Designed for clips, streams, friend stories, and “you had to be there” multiplayer disasters.
